# Brand Colors: PocketLedger

PocketLedger relies on a simple, trusted, and energetic palette tailored for financial clarity.

## Primary
**Emerald Green: #10B981**
- Use for primary CTAs, positive numbers (Income), and active states.
- High trust factor; implies growth and cash.

## Secondary
**Midnight Blue: #0F172A**
- Use for backgrounds, headers, and major text elements.
- Implies deep focus, privacy, and security.

## Accent
**Soft Gold: #FBBF24**
- Use for premium feature highlights, alerts, and subtle visual sparks.

## Whitespace
Ample use of pure white (#FFFFFF) and slate (#F8FAFC) should be deployed across cards to guarantee legibility and contrast against the Midnight Blue texts without visual overwhelm.
