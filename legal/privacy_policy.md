# Privacy Policy for PocketLedger

**Effective Date: April 11, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how PocketLedger ("we", "us", or "our") handles your personal information in our mobile application.

## 1. Zero Cloud Data Collection
PocketLedger is designed as an **Offline-First** application. We do not maintain any external servers to store your financial data. 
- **Account-Free**: No login or user account is required to use the app.
- **Financial Logs**: All transaction titles, amounts, and categories are stored locally on your device's internal storage.
- **No Transmission**: We do not transmit, upload, or sync your financial information to any third-party or our own servers.

## 2. Permissions & Usage
To provide the app's core functionality, we may request the following permissions:
- **Files & Media**: Only used if you explicitly choose to **Backup to JSON** or **Export to CSV**. You have full control over where these files are stored.
- **Biometrics**: Used locally to secure the app launch. Biometric data never leaves the secure hardware element of your device.
- **Notifications**: Used for local daily reminders (No data is sent over the internet).

## 3. Data Portability & Safety
Since we do not store your data, you have absolute control over it.
- **Backup/Restore**: You can export your entire database to a JSON file for backup and restore it on another device. This process is 100% user-initiated and local.
- **Deletion**: You can delete all your data at any time using the "Flush All Data" function in settings.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: **support@pocketledger.app**
