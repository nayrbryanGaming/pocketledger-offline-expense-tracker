# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

## 1. Introduction
PocketLedger ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. Our application is built with a strict "Offline-First" philosophy.

## 2. Data Collection and Storage
PocketLedger does **not** collect, transmit, or store your financial data on any external servers. All data (transactions, categories, budgets, and settings) is stored **locally** on your device using SQLite databases.

## 3. Data Syncing and Backup
Any backups generated (e.g., JSON export) are stored locally on your device or wherever you explicitly choose to save or share them. We do not have access to these files.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that harvest your personal financial information.

## 5. Security
While we provide options like Biometric Lock to secure the app locally, the overall security of your device's storage is your responsibility.

## 6. Changes to this Policy
We may update this policy. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 7. Contact Us
For privacy-related inquiries, please contact the repository owner on GitHub.
