# PocketLedger Product Strategy & KPIs

## Initial Target Users
1. **Students**: Needing to track allowance against strict monthly budgets.
2. **Freelancers**: Logging variable incomes and project-specific expenses.
3. **Small Business Owners**: Simple cashbook tracking offline in rural/retail environments without internet access.

## Growth Strategy
- **Product Hunt Launch**: Highlighting the "Privacy-First" & "Speed" angles.
- **SEO Blog**: Creating simple spreadsheet alternative guides.
- **TikTok**: Demonstrating "How I budget my freelance income without giving companies my data".

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Daily Installs**: Target 500/day within month 3.
- **7-day Retention**: Target >45% (finance apps typically struggle, offline speed is our hook).
- **Monthly Active Users**: Target 10,000 MAU.
- **Premium Conversion**: 3-5% of MAU purchasing the lifetime CSV/Backup tier.
